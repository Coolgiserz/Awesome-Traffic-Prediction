## New York City Taxi

#### Data Description

New York City taxi trip data including pickup/dropoff locations, timestamps, fares, and passenger information from yellow and green taxi services.

>纽约市出租车行程数据，包括上车/下车地点、时间戳、车费和乘客信息。
